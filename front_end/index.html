Recover Your Bike!

Recover Your Bike! is a community-based web application to reconnect stolen bikes and their owners in the Denver Metro area. Users may browse through list of currently-reported stolen bikes as well as submit the information on a bike they have had stolen. Working together, we as a community can reunite bikes and owners and thwart the no-good human beings that interfere with this most important relationship!
